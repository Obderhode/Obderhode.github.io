About WROC

OUR PURPOSE: To edify and equip women to be hands of love to the community in which they live and beyond. We will be committed to Biblical values so that our works won’t be swayed by those whose intentions may not be pure. Our hope is that women, who want to serve the Lord by making a difference in the lives of others, will join together and do great works, large and small.

OUR MANTRA: “For God is working in you, giving you the desire and the power to do what pleases him”. Phil 2:13
