About Re-New

There were many projects we wanted to accomplish and though God always provided what was necessary, we knew we needed to have a way to fund the ministry so we could reach further and be prepared when needs arose. Thus Re-New came into existence. Cindy Collier had many years of experience as a business owner and felt this could be a viable way to support WROC.

  • Household Items
  • Decor
  • Garden Items
